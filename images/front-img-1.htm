Embracing Handmade Art: Discover the Top 5 Handicraft Stores in Mumbai

61 views
Comment (0)

In a world full of machine-made items, there's something captivated many hearts for centuries- Handicraft items. What makes handicraft items special is the talents and art poured by the artisans. Art like pottery, handwoven blankets, handmade jewellery, and quilts stitched by hand hold a special place in the hearts of art lovers. Handicraft involves processing materials by hand using tools, resulting in useful or decorative items. Materials used in handicrafts are natural, industrially processed, or recycled, which adds more value to the talents of the artisans, like the concept of 'Best out of Waste'. Handicraft is deeply rooted in society and helps preserve and transmit traditions. Artisans transfer cultural heritage, values, philosophy, fashion, and self-image through their products. Handicraft artisans possess technical knowledge of materials and work methods, relying on manual skills. If you're familiar with theme-based handicrafts, you'll appreciate how they encompass a wide array of captivating crafts. From needlework to tribal crafts, fibre and eco-friendly creations, fashion accessories, and festive decorations, these crafts offer a rich and comprehensive guide to diverse artistic endeavours.

Are you based in Mumbai and interested in exploring the world of handicraft items? If yes, then we suggest you search for 'handicraft shop near me' and you shall come across ample options. But if you want us to ease your search then read this piece. We've listed down the top 5 handicraft stores in Mumbai. You can head to these handicraft stores to shop for the upcoming festive season or to buy a few keepsakes. So, read to know more!

Fun Fact:
The word 'handicraft' comes from Old English handcrft, "skill of the hand," and was probably modelled after handiwork.

Mela: The Ethnic Store

Love ethnic art? If yes, then head to Mela, one of the top handicraft stores in Mumbai. Mela: The Ethnic Store is a unique handicraft shop in R City Mall in Ghatkopar located between two giants on the 2nd floor. It offers a wide range of ethnic handicraft items and features a collection of handcrafted jewellery for women and intricate ethnic bags with intricate designs. The prices of all products depend on the intricacy of the designs, making it worth your money if you plan to buy a couple of handicraft items. So, this festive and wedding season, head to Mela- The Ethnic Store and buy yourself unique ethnic handicraft items made by artisans with love!

Creative Handicrafts

Creative Handicrafts is an initiative to support less fortunate women and empower them socially. This organisation is involved in ethical garment manufacturing, handicraft items, and homemade freshly cooked meals. With decades of experience, women artisans at Creative Handicrafts offer quality, timely, and ethical production of handmade, sustainable products. It is one of the oldest handicraft stores in Mumbai that offers a wide range of products with stylish designs at affordable rates. If you're looking to buy unique handicraft items made with love for yourself or your home, then this place is a must-visit!

Bharatkala Handicraft Bazar

While searching for handicraft items online, we came across Bharat Kala Handicraft Bazaar, one of the renowned handicraft stores in Mumbai. Bharat Kala Handicraft Bazaar is a top player in manufacturing Mirakhi folding mats from materials like high-quality foam, korai grass, and pure table print cotton. You can head to their handicraft shop in Malad and explore the many options available for foldable mirakhi mats for your homes. The local artisans handmade these mirakhi foldable mats that can be easily carried while travelling too. So, go explore the world of beautifully handcrafted foldable mirakhi mats.

Rajasthan Hastkala

If you love needlework on garments and handmade embellishments on jewellery, then you will fall in love with the handicraft items available at Rajasthan Hastkala. One of the best handicraft stores in Mumbai, Rajasthan Hastkala is a shopping paradise with endless surprises. This handicraft shop offers a wide variety of handmade apparel, jewellery, and home decor pieces at reasonable rates. You'll be spoilt for choices when it comes to upholstery and home furnishings as they are handmade with love by the Rajasthani artisans. The shopkeepers are always willing to show more options, as they have a large collection in store. So, heading to a handicraft shop like Rajasthan Hastkala that showcases regional art is a great choice!

Su:riti - The Culture Shop

If you've been searching for contemporary handicraft items in Mumbai, then your search ends here! Su:riti - The Culture Shop features over 21,000 contemporary arts and crafts from artisans across India. Located in Hiranandani Gardens in Powai, this handicraft shop also serves as a handicraft online store that promotes dying art forms and is popular with locals and foreign tourists due to its Indian theme. The wide range of handicraft items at this store is beautifully arranged in a way that exudes a pristine ambience and amazing vibe. You must visit this handicraft shop not just to explore their collection but to also feel the beautiful vibe!

Handicraft items stand out for their uniqueness, personal touch, and superior quality compared to mass-produced goods. Skillfully crafted by artisans, these items beautifully mirror the artisan's personality and creativity, presenting a refreshing and distinctive alternative to factory-made products. Handicraft items are made using traditional techniques, ensuring each piece is made to the highest standard. In this case, only high-quality materials are used, making them durable and long-lasting. This article has explored the significance of handicraft items and a few prominent handicraft stores in Mumbai. If handicrafts make you go 'wow' every time you see them then you must head to the above-listed handicraft stores. Handicraft items truly hold unique significance in our daily lives.
